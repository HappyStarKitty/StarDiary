傅里叶级数

$$
\frac{a_0}{2}+\sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}(a_n \cos \frac{n\pi x}{l}+b_n \sin \frac{n\pi x}{l})
$$

$$
\left\{
\begin{aligned}
a_n=\frac{1}{l}\int_{-l}^{l} f(x)\cos\frac{n\pi x}{l}dx, \quad n=0,1,2... \\
b_n=\frac{1}{l}\int_{-l}^{l} f(x)\sin\frac{n\pi x}{l}dx, \quad n=1,2,3...
\end{aligned}
\right.
$$
