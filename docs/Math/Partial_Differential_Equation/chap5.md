# Green函数法

## Laplace方程第一边值的Green函数法

**Green第一公式**

$$\iiint_\Omega u\Delta v d\Omega =\iint_{\partial \Omega} u\frac{\partial v}{\partial n}dS-\iiint_\Omega grad u \cdot grad v d\Omega$$

**Green第二公式**

$$\iiint_\Omega (u\Delta v-v\Delta u)d\Omega=\iint_{\partial \Omega}(u\frac{\partial v}{\partial n}-v\frac{\partial u}{\partial n})dS$$
